Domiciliary Care Services

Our Domiciliary Care Services are designed to support individuals who prefer to receive care in the comfort and familiarity of their own homes. We offer comprehensive home-based assistance, including personal care, medication administration, and meaningful companionship, all delivered by trained and compassionate professionals. Whether clients require help with daily routines, mobility support, or simply someone to talk to, our team is committed to enhancing their quality of life while promoting independence and dignity. Services are tailored to each individual’s needs and can range from short-term recovery support to long-term care arrangements. With a strong focus on safety, reliability, and respect, we ensure that every person receives attentive, professional care that aligns with their lifestyle and preferences.

Service Benefits

Whether it's short-term recovery or long-term assistance, domiciliary care offers a flexible and compassionate solution for those who value dignity, familiarity, and continuity.

What people usually ask

  • What kind of workers do you supply?

    We provide trained, vetted domiciliary care professionals including care assistants, support workers, and live-in carers. Our staff are experienced in personal care, medication support, mobility assistance, and companionship services.

  • Are your workers qualified and compliant with regulations?

    Yes. All care staff meet the standards set by the Care Quality Commission (CQC) — the independent regulator of health and social care in England. Workers are vetted through enhanced DBS checks, and we follow Regulation 19 of the Health and Social Care Act 2008 (Regulated Activities) Regulations 2014, which ensures all staff are “fit and proper persons”.

  • Can you provide staff on short notice?

    Yes. We maintain a flexible staffing pool to respond quickly to urgent needs — whether it’s sickness cover, holiday gaps, or increased demand.

  • How do you ensure quality and continuity?

    We match staff based on your care setting, client needs, and team culture. We also conduct regular performance reviews and offer replacements if needed.

  • Do you supply staff for specialist care?

    Yes. We have professionals trained in dementia care, end-of-life support, mental health services, and learning disabilities.
