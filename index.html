About Us

Established in 2019, HonourHealthcare Ltd is a trusted healthcare staffing provider, specialising in Residential Care Activities for Mental Difficulties, Mental Health, and Substance Abuse. We supply Registered Nurses, Healthcare Assistants, Support Workers, and Personal Assistants across the UK — delivering compassionate, reliable, and fully vetted professionals to meet your care needs.

Domiciliary Care Services

Our Domiciliary Care Services are designed to support individuals in the comfort of their own homes, helping them maintain independence while receiving the care they need. From personal care and medication support to companionship and daily living assistance, our trained carers deliver flexible, tailored services that respect dignity and promote well-being. Whether for a few hours a day or ongoing long-term care, we’re here to provide reliable support for you or your loved ones.
